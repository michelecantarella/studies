Before you begin

Thank you for your interest in our study! Please take the time to read the following text carefully. By pressing the consent button below, you confirm that you have read the information presented and that you are willing to participate in the study.

This study focuses on your experience managing earnings and time, and is divided into two modules: a short questionnaire, which will take less than 5 minutes to complete and will earn you a fixed payment upon completion, and an experimental module, which will allow you to gain a random bonus payment.

▸ Consent form (click to read)

The data collected will be used for research purposes only. Any responses you provide will be completely anonymous, as no identifying information will be collected during the study. Prolific will allow us to link your choices to your gender, age, and other demographics, but we will receive no identifying information about you, and we will not be able to link this information to your name.

If you give us your permission by completing and submitting the study, we plan to discuss/publish the results in an academic forum. The study data, which does not contain any personal information, may be shared with cooperation partners for collaborative analysis and made publicly accessible via research databases or scientific publications. Please note that once study data are publicly accessible, their further distribution by others cannot be ruled out and falls beyond the area of influence or responsibility of the IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca. This study is conducted in accordance with the laws D. Lgs. 196/2003 and UE GDPR 679/2016 on data protection. The experimental protocol and the management of the study data has been approved by the Joint Ethical Committee of the Universities "Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna", "Scuola Normale Superiore", and "IMT Scuola Alti Studi Lucca" (N: n. 23/2026, April 30).

You can reach out to Michele Cantarella (michele.cantarella@imtlucca.it) if you have any questions related to this study or if you wish your response to be deleted.

Instructions

The clicking tasks

You will complete 5 sections. Each section offers a random cash bonus; you see the exact amount before each section begins. You earn it only by completing the main task.

Each section consists of a series of tasks. In each task a 5×5 grid of emoji symbols progressively reveals from left to right. Once fully revealed, click all instances of the target emoji shown above the grid. The task submits automatically after a short countdown.

The main task

The number of tasks needed to earn the reward is random. The highlighted red zone shows where the task can end — it could be any task in that range, with equal probability. The grey zone is the safe zone: the task cannot end there.

A stickman marks your current position on the timeline, always visible at the top of your screen:

The stickman advances right with each completed task. When it enters the red zone, the task may end at any moment.

The alternative offer

At a random point during a section, a switch button will activate on the left and the alternative sequence will appear on the timeline. A brief notification will pop up to alert you. The task keeps going — you can switch at any time by clicking the button.

Later, the section will pause to let you decide. You can switch to the alternative, continue the main task, or forfeit both.

The blue line shows the alternative sequence — a fixed, known number of tasks. At the pause, you have three options:

  • Continue: keep going on the main task (button stays visible)
  • Switch: move to the alternative sequence
  • Forfeit: abandon both
