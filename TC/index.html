Welcome

Thank you for your interest in our study! Please take the time to read the following text carefully. By pressing the button below and continuing with this study, you confirm that you have read the information presented and that you are willing to participate in the study.


This study consists of a short survey about your preferences. It will take a few minutes to complete and will grant you a fixed payment upon completion. This study is a research project of the IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca.

Consent form

The data collected will be used for research purposes only. Any responses you provide will be completely anonymous, as no identifying information will be collected during the study. Prolific will allow us to link your choices to your gender, age, and other demographics, but we will receive no identifying information about you, and we will not be able to link this information to your name.

If you give us your permission by completing and submitting the survey, we plan to discuss/publish the results in an academic forum. The study data, which does not contain any personal information, may be shared with cooperation partners for collaborative analysis and made publicly accessible via research databases or scientific publications. Please note that once study data are publicly accessible, their further distribution by others cannot be ruled out and falls beyond the area of influence or responsibility of the IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca. This study is conducted in accordance with the laws D. Lgs. 196/2003 and UE GDPR 679/2016 on data protection. The experimental protocol and the management of the study data has been approved by the Joint Ethical Committee of the Universities "Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna", "Scuola Normale Superiore", and "IMT Scuola Alti Studi Lucca" (N: n. 36/2023).

You can reach out to Michele Cantarella (michele.cantarella@imtlucca.it) if you have any questions related to this study or if you wish your response to be deleted.

By selecting "I consent" below, you agree to participate in this study, understand the information above, and consent to participate in this project. Your consent will be timestamped and stored alongside your Prolific ID. If you do not consent, select "I do not consent," and you will be redirected to Prolific. You can revoke your consent at any time by leaving the study before completion. Incomplete responses will be deleted once we have finished collecting responses.
